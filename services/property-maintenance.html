Frequently Asked

Lawn mowing & property maintenance questions

How often should I mow my lawn in Northeast Ohio?

Most Aurora and Chagrin Valley lawns thrive on weekly mowing from late April through October. We recommend a 3-inch cutting height for cool-season grasses common in this region.

Do you offer bi-weekly mowing instead of weekly?

Yes — bi-weekly is available for properties with slower-growing turf or when you're comfortable with slightly longer grass between visits. Weekly delivers a more polished look.

What's included in routine property maintenance?

Lawn mowing, edging along walkways and beds, blowing off all hardscape, and routine bed weed maintenance. Every visit, every time.

When do you start and end the mowing season?

We typically start mid-to-late April once the soil is workable, and run through late October / early November depending on the weather.
