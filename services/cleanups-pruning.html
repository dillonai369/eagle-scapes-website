Seasonal Cleanups

Every season has its work.

Northeast Ohio properties take a beating from leaves, dropped branches, salt, and overgrowth. We reset things — three times a year, on schedule.

  • Spring cleanup — leaf cleanup, bed prep, first mow
  • Mid-season cleanup — refresh and reset for peak summer
  • Fall cleanup — leaf removal, final bed prep, winterizing
  • Bed redefining and edging
  • Hedge, shrub, and ornamental tree pruning
  • Plant deadheading and trimming
  • Weed and debris removal
