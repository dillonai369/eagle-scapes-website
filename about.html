Our Story

Built by hand. Run by an owner.

Eagle Scapes is a small, locally owned landscape company started in Aurora in 2023 — built on craftsmanship, fairness, and a genuine love for the work.

Meet Nathan

The owner is the operator.

Nathan Meyer started working in the landscape industry at age 15. He fell in love with the physicality and creativity of outdoor work — and never looked back.

In 2023, after years of working under other companies, he started Eagle Scapes with a simple promise: every property would get the attention of an owner who actually cared. No subcontracted crews. No corner-cutting. Just clean, honest work by someone who shows up himself.

Nathan operates with strong values of fairness, integrity, respect, and flexibility — and a hard work ethic that shows up in every job, every visit, every season.

What We Stand For

Four values, every day.

Fairness

Honest pricing. Transparent quotes. No hidden fees, no upsells you don't need.

Integrity

If we say we'll be there at 8 AM, we're there at 8 AM. If we make a mistake, we own it and fix it.

Respect

Your property is your investment. We treat it like our own — every visit, every season.

Flexibility

Every property is different. We tailor our work to what your yard actually needs — not what's easiest for us.

Let's work together.

If our values line up with yours, we'd love to put together a quote for your property.
